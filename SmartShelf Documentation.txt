# SmartShelf

## The Operating System for Community Pharmarmacies

### One-Line Pitch

SmartShelf is an offline-first pharmacy operating system that helps community pharmacies prevent medicine stockouts, reduce expiry losses, and automate restocking in low-connectivity environments.

---

# 1. The Problem

Across Sierra Leone, many community pharmacies still manage inventory manually using notebooks, memory, or spreadsheets. During busy periods, pharmacists prioritize serving customers and often do not have time to update inventory records.

As a result:

* Pharmacies only discover medicine shortages after stock has already run out.
* Patients experience treatment delays when essential medicines are unavailable.
* Pharmacy owners lose revenue due to missed sales opportunities.
* Medicines expire on shelves because slow-moving inventory is difficult to track.
* Restocking decisions are reactive and rely on manual checks, phone calls, and guesswork.

Existing pharmacy management systems are often expensive, overly complex, and dependent on stable internet connectivity, making them impractical for many local pharmacies.

---

# 2. The Solution

SmartShelf is an offline-first pharmacy operating system designed specifically for low-connectivity African markets.

The platform enables pharmacists to record medicine sales with a single tap, automatically track inventory levels, identify inventory risks before they become costly problems, and generate restock requests through familiar communication channels such as WhatsApp.

Instead of waiting until shelves become empty or products expire, SmartShelf continuously analyzes inventory activity and provides proactive recommendations that help pharmacies maintain medicine availability while reducing operational losses.

# 3. Hackathon MVP Scope

## A. One-Tap Sales Logging

A mobile-first interface designed for high-traffic pharmacy environments.

### Features

* One-tap medicine sales recording
* Large touch-friendly medicine tiles
* Optimized for phones and tablets
* Minimal training required
* Fully functional offline

### Example

Pharmacist taps:

* Coartem
* Amoxicillin
* Paracetamol

Inventory updates instantly.

---

## B. Offline Inventory Management

SmartShelf operates even without internet access.

### Features

* Real-time stock deduction
* Offline transaction storage
* Local-first architecture
* Automatic synchronization when connectivity returns

This ensures pharmacies can continue operating regardless of network conditions.

---

## C. Inventory Risk Intelligence

SmartShelf continuously monitors inventory and identifies risks before they impact operations.

### Stockout Risk Detection

The system analyzes inventory movement and estimates when a medicine is likely to run out.

#### Example

> ⚠️ Coartem is projected to run out in 4 days.

### Expiry Risk Detection

The system identifies medicines that are unlikely to be sold before their expiration date.

#### Example

> ⚠️ 15 packs of Amoxicillin may expire within 30 days.

### Features

* Consumption trend analysis
* Remaining stock calculations
* Projected stockout estimation
* Expiry date monitoring
* Slow-moving inventory detection
* Potential loss estimation
* Early warning notifications

---

## D. Automated Restock Assistant

When inventory reaches critical levels, SmartShelf generates supplier-ready reorder requests.

### Features

* Low-stock threshold monitoring
* Suggested reorder quantities
* WhatsApp order generation
* Supplier-ready purchase requests

### Example

**Reorder Request**

* Coartem × 50
* Paracetamol × 100
* Metronidazole × 30

The request can be sent directly through WhatsApp.

---

## E. Pharmacy Insights Dashboard

A lightweight operational dashboard that provides immediate visibility into inventory performance.

### Features

* Fast-moving medicines
* Slow-moving medicines
* Inventory risk alerts
* Stockout forecasts
* Expiry-risk forecasts
* Inventory health overview
